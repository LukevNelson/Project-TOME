The Original Vampire

ü©∏ True Identity

Vlad Dracula was not merely a historical figure - he was the Original Vampire, the first of his kind created by the Primordial of Blood. For millennia, he has walked among mortals, studying their evolution, shaping their progress, and occasionally revealing himself to test how legends are born and spread.

üß¨ Primordial Heritage

Created directly by the Primordial of Blood, Vlad possesses divine essence that makes him fundamentally different from all other vampires. He is not just the first - he is the template from which all vampire abilities derive.

‚ö° Unique Abilities

Unlike his descendants, Vlad can walk in sunlight, manipulate electrokinetic forces, and has perfect recall of every moment of his immortal existence. His powers have no known limits.

üìö The Great Observer

For over 6,000 years, Vlad has been humanity's silent chronicler, documenting societal evolution, technological advancement, and the rise and fall of civilizations.

üé≠ The Master of Legends

Vlad chose to live as the historical Vlad III of Wallachia not out of necessity, but as a calculated experiment. He wanted to understand how mortals react to legendary figures, how fear spreads, and how myths are born from reality.

"What happens when a monster reveals himself to the world? Do they flee, fight, or create stories that outlast the truth itself? I had to know." - Vlad's personal journals

Lysandra and the Mona Lisa

In his thousands of years of existence, Vlad found true love only once. Lysandra (sometimes called Lisandra) was a mortal woman who not only discovered his vampiric nature but embraced it. Their relationship spanned several of her lifetimes through magical intervention, but mortality ultimately claimed her.

üåÖ First Meeting

Vlad encountered Lysandra during his time documenting the Renaissance. She was a scholar who recognized inconsistencies in his stories of "ancient times he had studied."

üîç The Discovery

Rather than flee when she learned his true nature, Lysandra became fascinated by his perspective on human history. She became his intellectual equal and emotional anchor.

‚ö∞Ô∏è The Final Goodbye

üé® Leonardo's Technique

üíî The Pain of Immortal Love

Vlad's relationship with Lysandra taught him the cruelest aspect of immortality - watching those you love age and die while you remain unchanged. This experience shaped his later interactions with mortals and his decision to eventually step back from direct human contact.

"She saw past the monster to the scholar beneath. In her eyes, I was not the creature who had witnessed empires fall, but simply the man who loved her. When she died, part of my humanity died with her." - From Vlad's private writings

üí° The Spark of Innovation

Vlad's greatest contribution to human advancement came through his understanding of electrical forces. As an Original Vampire he used his immortality to explore electrokinetic abilities and the sciences behind Light and electricity, he grasped the potential of electricity centuries before human science caught up.

üî¨ The Edison Partnership

In the late 1870s, Vlad approached Thomas Edison with a proposition. Edison, already experimenting with electrical devices, had hit numerous dead ends in creating a practical incandescent light bulb. Vlad provided the following crucial insights:

  • Filament Material: Suggested carbonized bamboo based on his (Vlad's) observations of natural electrical conductivity
  • Vacuum Technology: Shared knowledge of atmospheric manipulation learned from magical studies
  • Electrical Distribution: Proposed the concept of electrical grids based on his (Vlad's) understanding of energy flow

Edison's own electrokinetic abilities, awakened through exposure to Vlad's teachings, allowed him to test and refine these concepts with supernatural precision.

üìö The Hidden Archives

Throughout his millennia of observation, Vlad has maintained detailed records of human development. These archives represent the ultimate test of immortal cunning - they are hidden not just in location, but in time itself. Each era's secrets can only be unlocked using methods, knowledge, and technologies specific to that period. This creates an almost impossible puzzle that would require someone to be present at every crucial moment in history to even attempt to piece together the complete picture.

üß† The Immortal's Gambit

By the time they learned modern techniques, they would lack the foundational ancient knowledge. Only someone who lived through every era - or another immortal - could even attempt to unravel the complete picture.

"Let them try to piece together the puzzle. By the time they understand one era's secrets, three more have passed them by. I am not hiding from the present - I am hiding in plain sight across all of time itself.   • Immortal Cooperation: Seeking help from other long-lived supernatural beings who might have witnessed the same events

The irony is that Vlad's own continued involvement with T.O.M.E. means he occasionally provides subtle guidance to researchers attempting to unlock his own archives - though they remain unaware of their mysterious benefactor's true identity.

The Battle with Mehmet II

‚öîÔ∏è When Mortal Ambition Met Immortal Power

The conflict between Vlad and Sultan Mehmet II was far more than the historical records suggest. While the world saw a struggle between Wallachia and the Ottoman Empire, the truth was a confrontation between a mortal who discovered supernatural secrets and an immortal testing the limits of exposure.

üîç Mehmet's Discovery

Sultan Mehmet II was a brilliant military strategist and scholar who collected ancient texts from conquered territories. Among these texts were fragmentary references to "blood drinkers" and "the deathless ones." When his spies reported Vlad's impossible battlefield feats and his apparent immunity to aging, Mehmet began to suspect the truth.

üïµÔ∏è The Evidence Mehmet Gathered
  • Battlefield Reports: Vlad moved with inhuman speed and strength
  • Survivor Accounts: Stories of Vlad appearing in multiple locations simultaneously
  • Historical Inconsistencies: Records of "Vlad Dracul" dating back centuries before his supposed birth
  • Physical Evidence: Bloodstains that exhibited unusual properties

üëë The Sultan's Gambit

Rather than flee or seek supernatural protection, Mehmet saw opportunity. He believed that capturing or killing an Original would grant him not just glory, but potentially immortality itself. He began preparing for a confrontation that would be unlike any battle in recorded history.

üó°Ô∏è Blessed Weapons

Mehmet commissioned weapons blessed by multiple religious traditions, believing that divine power might wound an immortal.

üß™ Alchemical Research

Employed court alchemists to create substances that might weaken or bind a vampire, based on ancient texts.

üìú Scholarly Consultation

Gathered scholars from across the empire to study vampire lore and develop tactical advantages.

‚ö° Strategic Positioning

Chose battle locations with specific geographical features that might limit supernatural mobility.

üåô The Night Attack of 1462

What history records as Vlad's famous night attack on the Ottoman camp was actually Mehmet's trap being turned against him. The Sultan had deliberately positioned his forces to appear vulnerable, hoping to draw Vlad into a prepared ambush. Instead, he discovered why mortals should not challenge Originals.

üåÖ Sunset: The Approach

Vlad knew it was a trap but chose to engage anyway, seeing it as the perfect test of how a mortal would react when their supernatural theories proved correct.

üåô Midnight: The Confrontation

When Vlad reached Mehmet's tent, the Sultan was waiting with his blessed weapons and alchemical preparations. For a brief moment, mortal ambition faced immortal power in single combat.

‚ö° The Reckoning

Mehmet's preparations proved insufficient. Vlad's supernatural abilities overwhelmed the Sultan's earthly power, but rather than kill his opponent, Vlad chose to demonstrate mercy - and make a point about the futility of challenging the supernatural.

üî• The Camp's Destruction

Using his electrokinetic abilities, Vlad turned the Ottoman camp's own weapons against them, creating fires and explosions that seemed to come from nowhere.

üïäÔ∏è The Rescue of 10,000

During the chaos of battle, Vlad accomplished his true objective - rescuing the 10,000 boys that Mehmet had planned to use as leverage. This act of mercy, performed while demonstrating overwhelming power, became the foundation for one of history's most enduring legends.

"Let them remember not just my power, but my choices. A monster who saves children confounds their understanding of good and evil. This confusion will serve the legend well." - Vlad's tactical notes

The Birth of Alucard

‚öîÔ∏è Among the Rescued

Among the 10,000 boys rescued from Mehmet's camp was a young warrior whose life would be forever changed by witnessing true supernatural power. This boy, whose original name has been lost to history, would become the legendary vampire hunter known as Alucard.

üë¶ The Boy Who Saw Everything

Unlike the other rescued children, this boy remained conscious during the supernatural battle. He saw Vlad's true form, witnessed his electrokinetic abilities, and understood that the world contained powers beyond mortal comprehension. Rather than drive him mad, this revelation gave him purpose.

üëÅÔ∏è What He Witnessed
  • Transformation: Vlad shifting between human and vampiric forms
  • Supernatural Speed: Movement that defied physical laws
  • Electrokinetic Power: Lightning commanded by will alone
  • Mercy in Power: The choice to save rather than destroy

üé≠ The Name "Alucard"

When the boy came of age, he chose the name "Alucard" - Dracula spelled backwards. This wasn't mockery but profound respect. He saw himself as Vlad's opposite: where Vlad was immortal, he would be mortal; where Vlad was darkness, he would be light; where Vlad created vampires, he would hunt them.

üéØ The Hunter's Philosophy

Alucard didn't hunt vampires out of hatred but out of respect for what Vlad represented. He believed that if vampires were to exist in the world, they should live up to the standard set by their Original. Lesser vampires who preyed mindlessly on humans dishonored the nobility he had witnessed in their progenitor.

üìú Alucard's Code

  1. Judge by Actions: Vampires who showed restraint and wisdom were spared
  2. Protect the Innocent: Priority given to saving victims over destroying predators
  3. Honor the Original: Never pursue Vlad himself, recognizing the debt owed
  4. Teach Through Example: Show other hunters that discrimination was possible
  5. Preserve the Balance: Maintain the hidden nature of the supernatural world

üîó The Eternal Connection

Throughout his mortal life, Alucard never encountered Vlad again, but their connection transcended physical meeting. Alucard's reputation as a fair but deadly vampire hunter reached Vlad's ears, and the Original took pride in the boy's development. In a way, Alucard became Vlad's greatest success - proof that even supernatural power could inspire nobility rather than just fear.

"Of all the children I saved that night, only one truly understood what he witnessed. In becoming my opposite, he became my greatest legacy. The hunter who bears my name backwards carries my honor forward." - Vlad's reflection on Alucard

üìö The Legend Lives On

Alucard's descendants continued his tradition, creating a bloodline of vampire hunters who understood the complexity of the supernatural world. Unlike other hunters who saw only monsters, the Alucard lineage recognized that supernatural beings, like humans, could choose between good and evil.

üåü The Alucard Legacy

  • Training Methods: Developed techniques specifically for fighting vampires with honor
  • Diplomatic Relations: Established protocols for communication with "civilized" vampires
  • Historical Documentation: Maintained accurate records of supernatural encounters
  • Cultural Influence: Inspired stories that depicted vampires as complex beings rather than simple monsters

Modern Legacy

üåê The Digital Age Observer

In the 21st century, Vlad continues his role as humanity's chronicler, now fascinated by the digital revolution and its effects on human connection, privacy, and the spread of information. He maintains a presence in the modern world through carefully constructed identities and shell corporations.

üíº Contemporary Involvement

üè¢ Technology Investment

üéì Educational Foundations

üìö Digital Archives

üåç Global Monitoring

üèõÔ∏è T.O.M.E. Connection

Vlad views the emergence of T.O.M.E. (The Omniverse of Magical Education) with great interest. For the first time in millennia, humans are attempting to systematically study and understand the supernatural world. He provides anonymous guidance and funding, seeing this as potentially the next great leap in human evolution.

üìñ Hidden Contributions to T.O.M.E.

  • Safety Protocols: Anonymously submits safety guidelines for supernatural encounters
  • Technological Support: Provides advanced security systems to protect sensitive information
  • Strategic Misdirection: Ensures that dangerous information remains classified while educational content flourishes

üîÆ Future Observations

As humanity stands on the brink of potential space colonization and artificial intelligence integration, Vlad prepares for his next great observation period. He wonders how human nature will adapt to these challenges and what new forms of society might emerge.

üí≠ The Eternal Question

After six millennia of observation, Vlad continues to ponder the fundamental question that has driven his existence: What is humanity's true potential? He has seen them at their worst and their best, witnessed their capacity for both creation and destruction. As they stand on the threshold of becoming a space-faring, technologically integrated species, he wonders if they will finally transcend their limitations or if the patterns he has observed will simply repeat on a grander scale.

"I have watched empires rise and fall, seen humanity achieve the impossible and destroy the beautiful. Now they reach for the stars while still struggling with the same fears and desires that drove their ancestors. Perhaps immortality's greatest gift is not avoiding death, but having enough time to truly understand what it means to be alive." - Vlad's most recent journal entry
