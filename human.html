Human Overview

ğŸ§‘ What are Humans?

Humans are the most numerous and diverse species in the world. Most are unaware of the magical world, living ordinary lives governed by science and reason. However, some are drawn into the supernatural by fate, curiosity, or accident.

While physically and magically weaker than other species, humans possess adaptability, ingenuity, and the capacity for both great good and evil.

Humans form the backbone of mortal civilization, building cities, nations, and cultures. Their actions, beliefs, and discoveries often have ripple effects in the magical world, even if they remain unaware of it.
