Technical Specifications

iOS Requirements

iOS Version: iOS 14.0 or later
Devices: iPhone 8 or newer, iPad (6th gen) or newer
RAM: 3GB or more recommended
Storage: 2GB free space minimum
AR Features: ARKit compatible device

Android Requirements

Android Version: Android 7.0 (API level 24) or later
Architecture: arm64-v8a, armeabi-v7a
RAM: 3GB or more recommended
Storage: 2GB free space minimum
AR Features: ARCore supported device

Flutter Framework Benefits

Performance: Native-level performance on both platforms
Updates: Simultaneous iOS and Android releases
UI Consistency: Identical experience across platforms
Hot Reload: Rapid development and bug fixes
Codebase: Single codebase for all platforms
