Built with Flutter for seamless cross-platform performance, our mobile app brings the entire Skulduggery Pleasant universe to your fingertips. Whether you're a longtime fan or just discovering the series, this app provides everything you need to explore Derek Landy's magical world.

From detailed character biographies to interactive magic tutorials, from reading guides to trivia challenges, the app serves as your comprehensive companion for all things Skulduggery Pleasant. Available offline and regularly updated with new content.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app really free?

Yes! The Skulduggery Pleasant Companion App is completely free with no ads, in-app purchases, or subscription fees. We're passionate fans creating this for the community.

Does it work offline?

Absolutely! You can download all content for offline access. Perfect for reading on the go without worrying about data usage or internet connectivity.

How often is content updated?

We release updates monthly with new quiz questions, character information, and features. Major updates coincide with new book releases or significant series events.

Can I suggest features or report bugs?

Yes! We have an active feedback system within the app and you can also contact us through our Discord community or email. We love hearing from users!
