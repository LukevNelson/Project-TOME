An Unprecedented Magical Experience

For the first time ever, experience the Skulduggery Pleasant universe in full 3D glory. Our fan-made game brings Derek Landy's magical world to life with stunning visuals, authentic character interactions, and gameplay that truly captures the essence of being a sorcerer.

Built from the ground up in Unreal Engine 5, "Shadows of Magic" leverages the latest in gaming technology including Lumen global illumination, Nanite virtualized geometry, and advanced particle systems to create the most visually stunning representation of the magical world ever created.

Core Gameplay Features

Magic Combat System

Experience dynamic real-time magical combat with:

  • All 4 elemental disciplines (Fire, Water, Earth, Air)
  • 15+ adept disciplines including Necromancy and Energy-Throwing
  • Combo system for creating powerful spell combinations
  • Environmental destruction and interaction
  • Physics-based spell effects and reactions
