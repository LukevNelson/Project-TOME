Energy Throwing

Projectile Magic and Combat Arts

Intermediate Combat Common Offensive

Overview

Energy Throwing is one of the most popular and practical magical disciplines, focusing on projecting various forms of energy as weapons or tools. This combat-oriented discipline allows practitioners to hurl bolts of concentrated magical energy with devastating effect.

Unlike elemental magic that manipulates existing forces, energy throwing creates pure magical energy that can be shaped and directed according to the caster's will. This makes it an incredibly versatile discipline for both offensive and defensive applications.

Core Principles

  • Converting internal magical energy into projectiles
  • Precise targeting and trajectory control
  • Energy concentration and amplification
  • Rapid casting for combat situations
  • Energy efficiency and conservation
  • Defensive energy barrier creation

Combat Applications

Energy throwing is primarily a combat discipline with various tactical uses:

  • Direct Attack: Concentrated energy bolts for maximum damage
  • Suppressive Fire: Rapid, low-power shots to control area
  • Precision Strikes: Accurate shots for specific targets
  • Area Denial: Creating energy barriers or fields
  • Non-Lethal Force: Stunning or incapacitating opponents
  • Utility Functions: Cutting, welding, or illumination

Types of Energy Projection

Standard Energy Bolt

Power: Medium | Speed: Fast

The most common form of energy throwing. Concentrated magical energy formed into a projectile for direct impact damage.

Characteristics:

  • Reliable and consistent
  • Good balance of power and speed
  • Suitable for most combat situations
  • Easy to aim and control

Charged Blast

Power: High | Speed: Slow

A highly concentrated energy attack that takes time to charge but delivers devastating damage to targets.

Characteristics:

  • Maximum damage potential
  • Requires charging time
  • Effective against armored targets
  • High energy consumption

Rapid Fire

Power: Low | Speed: Very Fast

Quick succession of low-power energy bolts for suppressive fire and area control.

Characteristics:

  • High rate of fire
  • Good for suppression
  • Lower individual damage
  • Efficient energy usage

Precision Strike

Power: Medium | Speed: Medium

Highly accurate energy bolts designed for hitting specific targets or weak points with surgical precision.

Characteristics:

  • Extreme accuracy
  • Focused damage
  • Requires concentration
  • Effective against critical points

Energy Barrier

Power: Variable | Speed: Instant

Defensive application creating walls or shields of energy to block incoming attacks.

Characteristics:

  • Defensive capability
  • Variable strength
  • Temporary duration
  • Energy-intensive to maintain

Guided Projectile

Power: Medium | Speed: Variable

Advanced technique allowing energy bolts to change direction mid-flight to track targets.

Characteristics:

  • Target tracking ability
  • Complex to control
  • High accuracy potential
  • Requires advanced training

Combat Techniques and Tactics

Offensive Tactics

  • Direct Assault: Concentrated fire on primary targets
  • Suppressive Fire: Keeping enemies pinned down
  • Flanking Attacks: Using mobility to attack from angles
  • Overwhelming Force: Multiple simultaneous attacks
  • Precision Strikes: Targeting weak points or leaders

Defensive Tactics

  • Energy Barriers: Creating protective shields
  • Counter-attacks: Responding to incoming threats
  • Covering Fire: Protecting allies during movement
  • Area Denial: Preventing enemy advancement
  • Emergency Escape: Blast-assisted retreat

Team Coordination

  • Focused Fire: Multiple throwers targeting one enemy
  • Layered Defense: Coordinated barrier placement
  • Cover and Advance: Alternating movement and fire
  • Crossfire Patterns: Overlapping fields of fire
  • Combined Arms: Working with other disciplines

Essential Training Exercises

Target Practice

Fundamental accuracy training using stationary and moving targets at various distances.

Objectives:

  • Improve accuracy
  • Develop muscle memory
  • Practice range estimation
  • Build confidence

Power Control

Learning to modulate energy output for different situations, from stunning to maximum damage.

Objectives:

  • Energy regulation
  • Efficiency improvement
  • Non-lethal options
  • Stamina conservation

Speed Drills

Rapid-fire exercises to improve casting speed and reaction times in combat situations.

Objectives:

  • Faster casting
  • Quick targeting
  • Combat reflexes
  • Stress management

Barrier Training

Defensive exercises focusing on creating and maintaining energy barriers under pressure.

Objectives:

  • Defensive capabilities
  • Barrier durability
  • Resource management
  • Protection tactics

Combat Scenarios

Simulated combat situations to practice tactics, teamwork, and decision-making under pressure.

Objectives:

  • Tactical thinking
  • Team coordination
  • Stress testing
  • Real-world application

Endurance Training

Extended practice sessions to build magical stamina and maintain effectiveness over long periods.

Objectives:

  • Stamina building
  • Sustained combat
  • Fatigue management
  • Consistency maintenance
